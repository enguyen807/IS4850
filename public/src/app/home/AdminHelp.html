  • How to Reward Loyalty Points
  1. Click on the “Reservation” button in the upper right-hand corner of the Blue Ocean Website.
  2. Fill out the fields marked with a *, these are mandatory. (If you are logged in, the contact fields will be filled in for you.)
    1. NOTE: If you have already created an account, but are not logged in, click the blue Sign in tab to avoid filling out the name and contact information.
  3. Select a date and time using the calendar fields near the bottom of the form.
  4. Choose the number of guests (1-5) in your party using the drop-down menu in the bottom right.
    1. NOTE: If your reservation is for more than 5 people we request that you call in advance.
  5. Click the large “Create reservation” button to submit.
  6. You will see notification on the screen that an email has been sent to you account.
  7. Check the email account you entered for confirmation.
  1. After you login with the administrative credentials, choose “account management” from the dropdown menu with your account name on it.
  2. From here you can see registered users in the center of the screen, and any active reservations below that.
  3. Under the word “Reservations” there are two tabs. The tab that comes up by default is labelled “Users” and will show reservations made by customers with accounts.
  4. To view reservations created by customers without accounts, click on the “Guests” tab. Clicking on these tabs will toggle back and forth between user reservations and guest reservations.
  1. After you login with your credentials, choose “account management” from the dropdown menu with your account name on it.
  2. Any active reservations will be displayed here.
  3. How to Cancel:
    1. Click the gray “cancel” button next to the reservation you wish to cancel.
    2. A popup will appear asking confirming your actions.
    3. Clicking “Yes” will delete the reservation. Clicking “No” will keep the reservation intact.
  4. How to Edit:
    1. Click the gray “edit” button next to the reservation.
    2. Change the necessary information.
    3. Click the “OK” button to confirm the changes.
