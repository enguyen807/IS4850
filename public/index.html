ABOUT US

Affordable Authentic Japanese Food

Two roommates, MBA graduates, agreed at a coffee shop in the Delmar Loop "Why is it so hard for us as Asian students to find a good Japanese food in the Mid West, yet affordable?". They spent years in college cooking at their own apartment to impress colleagues along with studying supply chain, they opened Blue Ocean Sushi in 2008, next door to the Delmar Loop hot spot, the 609 Lounge. Named after a business book, with a believe to provide exceptional Japanese dining experience to the Washington University community in the Delmar Loop. Unlike others, Blue Ocean Sushi was originally serving Omakase style where customers just sat down and be fed by the chef. Then in 2009, it offered All You Can Eat Sushi where customers can enjoy selections of sushi with controllable budget. It was a very good feedback! Later in 2011, with more focus on other comfort food like Ramen, Tsukemen, Soba, Udon, Gyudon, Chashudon, it became "Blue Ocean Restaurant".


Named the best Ramen in Missouri by Foursquare.com, thanks to our locally made ramen noodle that is made from Missouri wheat. Blue Ocean is where you can find a variety of Japanese quick fix or traditional and creative sushi rolls. Our menu updates seasonally and aims to bring original flavor to your table.
